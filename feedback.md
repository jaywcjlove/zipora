Feedback & Support  
===  

Zipora is a native macOS compression and decompression tool developed using Swift / SwiftUI. It is designed for daily desktop scenarios, supporting archive creation, previewing archive contents, and extracting to specific directories. It leverages native macOS interactions such as drag-and-drop import, file pickers, Finder location, and recent items.

Your feedback and support are invaluable! If you encounter any issues, have questions, or would like to suggest new features, please feel free to reach out. Your input helps us continuously improve `Zipora` and ensures we deliver the best possible user experience.  

You can contact us through the in-app feedback feature or by sending an email to **kennyiseeyou@gmail.com**. We are dedicated to providing timely support and ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for you. Thank you for choosing Zipora!
