## Service Overview

**Zipora** is a native macOS compression and decompression tool developed using Swift / SwiftUI. It is designed for daily desktop scenarios, supporting archive creation, previewing archive contents, and extracting to specific directories. It leverages native macOS interactions such as drag-and-drop import, file pickers, Finder location, and recent items. The App operates offline, and all functions are performed locally without relying on an internet connection.
